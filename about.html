I share all the knowledge that I acquired during my business life with the participants.

I try to offer the latest software development tools to anyone with fond through the seminars-conferences that I organize at universities and international events.

As a result of these activities, I've seen worthy of the titles; 'Most Valuable Professional (MVP)' and 'Regional Director (RD)' by Microsoft. Through my knowledge of the Oracle Java, I can look at broader perspectives than a developer who knows only .Net or C# and I can find different solutions to problems.

Since 2014, I have Microsoft Certified Trainer certificate also which means Microsoft's consultant and instructor with certificate.

For my other certificates, activities, job titles and awards you may visit http://www.enginpolat.com/about/ page.

In effect of my master's degree courses (Next 5 Year in IT, etc.) , I'm following closely the IT areas which became trend in recent years, and I do studies/shares.

My works that I've done as Cloud computing (especially Microsoft Azure and Amazon Web Services) and in Mobile field (in particular Windows Phone and Android), opened the door of a lot of seminar at home and abroad as a speaker / jury.
